About

My Story

A creative at heart, seeking inspiration and inspiring others has driven my curiosity and creative skills. After years of developing my artistic abilities I started my own full-time design business. With a focus on animation and typography I tackled any of my client’s design problems, including illustration, websites, merchandise, and branding. After three years of coming up with imaginative solutions for client’s design needs I wanted to expand my skill set, which led me to obtaining a certification for UI from CareerFoundry. I’m looking for a home where my diverse skills and talents can continue to be inventive, turning ideas into products that are visually stunning and delightful to use.
